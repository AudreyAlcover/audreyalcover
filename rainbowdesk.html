Rainbow Serpent Festival is a 4 days annual open air of electronic music where you can have Psytrance, techno and world musique alternative.
Is not only a open air of music but is also a exposition of art, of a lifestyle and a place where everything weird is normal, like drive a sofa or disguise himself as an Aladdin’s genius.
Sleep in a tente, roof tente, in the middle of the stage, in a hammock, in your car or in a tippy, everything is safe and good to have a rest.
You have many food truck and market stall goodies comming from all around the world, its a new hippie vibes. The rainbow serpent festival is waiting 12,000 approximately people each year.
