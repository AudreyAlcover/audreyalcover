UX research : Persona & user journey
I find at the end that “Laura”my persona start to be frustrated at the first step of find a place to book the event and leave the web page because she don’t have any feedbacks and decide to organize by herself the event.

That took me to the next step and wirte a problem statement:

“ We have observed that Laura, who wants to organize the birthday of her best friend, finds good offers online but she never gets the proper support and guidance from the event website, so that she feel frustrated and doesn’t finish her booking. HMW reassure her during the booking process so that she doesn’t leave the website at any step of the process ?”

What is RoomBâ ?


Imagined by Justine Ba, RoomBâ will be a web site to find and organize your event from A to Z with a real transparency of the final price but most important about the availability date of booking the place.

Is a big challenge to accomplish because the town hall of city are not well organized to delivered fast the official confirmation.
Is also complicated to book a bar and privatise the place when you are not sure about how many people or find the good place for the right event.
